    深度學習於音樂生成與分析課程期末專題（楊奕軒老師授課）


    GUITAR AUDIO GENERATION VIA MUSICGEN: FINE-TUNING APPROACH FOR HIGH-QUALITY GUITAR SYNTHESIS
    Authors: Jih-Ming Bai, I-Pei Lee, Ting-An Yin

    This study explores fine-tuning the MusicGen model to generate high-quality guitar music. Using the GuitarSet dataset, we applied LoRA technology and data augmentation strategies to optimize model training. Two training strategies were tested: incorporating special tokens and enriching text prompts. Our results demonstrate that finetuning significantly improves the generated audio, with descriptive text prompts playing a crucial role in guiding the model to produce better guitar music. Although the finetuned model captures distinct guitar timbre and style, challenges remain in generating realistic melodies. This research offers valuable insights into AI-driven guitar music generation and suggests that refining training strategies and expanding datasets could further enhance model performance, providing a foundation for future advancements in this area.

    魚定勝天—斑馬魚在競爭過程中採取之評估模式

    本實驗經由討論斑馬魚在隨機配對及體長相近下的競爭，了解斑馬魚在競爭過程中的評估模式。斑馬魚在同性別或不同性別競爭下，皆採取「相互評估」，且體長、重量對斑馬魚的競爭能力有正面影響；而體高、尾鰭長則是負面影響。在同性別競爭下，雄魚群與雌魚群的階級決定時間無顯著差異，然而不同性別的競爭下，雌魚的形態特徵比雄魚對階級決定時間有更大的影響。
